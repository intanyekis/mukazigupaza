How Did Davina Potratz From 'Selling Sunset' Earn Her Incredible Net Worth?

June 2024 · 5 minute read

Davina Potratz wears many hats: actress, model, social media influencer, and most importantly, real estate agent. Fans have come to know the villainous recurring cast member (whether Potratz is mean behind the scenes is a different story entirely) of Selling Sunset quite well, as the loathed reality star has made her mark on the hit reality show. Through various endeavors, Potratz has amassed a net worth of $2 million (though her net worth isn't as impressive as show creator Adam DiVello’s). But how did the Selling Sunset star amass her fortune?

RELATED: ‘Selling Sunset’: Amanza Smith Says How She Met Taye Diggs After Her Divorce

Davina has managed to acquire a hefty bit of wealth for herself over the years. A shrewd business sense and a little notoriety have ensured that the 31-year-old Hamburg native is sitting not only on a nice chunk of change, but has also cemented her villainous persona in the minds of Selling Sunset’s loyal and devoted fan base. Let’s take a gander at Potratz road to riches, shall we?

6 Davina Potratz Started Out As A Model

Long before Selling Sunset and before she became a solid presence on social media, Davina was a model. After obtaining her Bachelor's Degree in advertisement, Potratz moved on to sign a modelling contract with the Ford Modelling agency back in 1999. It seems the future real estate agent was drawn to the spotlight from the very beginning.

5 Davina Potratz Made A Few Stops Before Making Her Real Estate Debut

Davina began to branch out from the modelling world, becoming an editorial assistant at Flaunt Magazine for a year before leaving in April 2000. Potratz then moved on to Angeleno Magazine, becoming a contributing editor. From there, Davina transitioned into the world of Sales Agent, working for a plethora of firms including Corcoran Sunshine Marketing Group, The Ryness Company, AEG, The Mark Company, HEI Hotels & Resorts among others. Just a taste of things to come in terms of the world of sales.

4 Davina Potratz Then Moved On To Both The Big And Little Screen

Transitioning from modelling to the silver screen isn't an absolute certainty. After all, acting and modelling require a different skill set. However, both professions are suited for anyone who dreams of basking in the public eye. Davina successfully made the transition from the world of modelling to Hollywood. According to IMDb, Potratz has been featured in such films as the Jennifer Lopez vehicle Monster-in-Law, an uncredited role as herself in the TV documentary On The Set: Raising Hell and of course making a strong TV presence on Selling Sunset, debuting on the series’ third season. Undoubtedly, Potratz stint on the movie set has done much to add to her bank account.

3 Davina Potratz Worked For The Oppenheim Group For Quite A While

Finally, making her real estate debut, becoming an agent with Lowe Enterprises and Etco Homes. Potratz then became the Principal, New Development Sales and Marketing agent with The Davina Group until eventually finding her home at The Oppenheim Group. In 2018, Potratz became Director of New Development/Broker Associate. After making her debut on Selling Sunset, fans became very familiar with the villainous member of the illustrious Oppenheim Group, agent. Of course, change seems to be at the heart of what drives Davina, so it comes as no surprise to fans of the show when she made her exit from the very organization that made her famous.

2 Davina Potratz Then Moved On To Douglas Elliman

Having to deal with the rigors of a reality show (such as rules that the cast of the show must follow) were not enough to stop Davina from having a change of heart and moving on from The Oppenheim Group to Douglas Elliman. According to People Magazine, Potratz said this about joining Douglas Elliman, "I'm really excited to be a part of Douglas Elliman and their very sophisticated and global new development division. My background is in new development sales and marketing, so this is just a wonderful opportunity for me. It's just very in tune with my skills." Despite her exit from The Oppenheim Group, there is no hard feelings from founder, Jason Oppenheim. According to People magazine, Jason had this to say, "Davina has always been a tremendous asset and a valued agent at the Oppenheim Group, and she is also a friend whose career decisions I very much respect and support. I will always want the best for Davina, and I hope that we continue to work together in the future."

1 And Of Course, There’s Davina Potratz's Pending $75 Million House Sale

Potratz is a salesperson first and foremost and adding a pricey Hollywood mansion to your portfolio is certainly a salesperson’s crowning achievement. As of this writing, Davina has yet to sell the $75 million Hollywood mansion and has even received backlash from Netflix about it (to which she sarcastically responded). However, when the property is sold, that will undoubtedly be a rather large commission and thus add to her already hefty net worth. Good luck, Ms. Potratz.
