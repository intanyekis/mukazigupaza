What Is Billy Porter’s Net Worth?

In 2020, “Time” magazine included Billy on its list of the world’s 100 most influential people. With over 30 acting credits to his name, Porter is a true icon in the entertainment industry.

Early Life and Education

Billy Porter was born on September 21, 1969, in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania. He grew up in a very religious Pentecostal household and attended Reizenstein Middle School, Allderdice High School, and Pittsburgh Creative and Performing Arts School. During his summers from 1985 to 1987, Billy performed with the entertainment groups Flash and Spirit at the Kennywood amusement park.
