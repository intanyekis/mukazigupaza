How did the Newfield Accident happen?

The troopers said the investigation revealed that a Jeep Patriot and a Toyota Yaris were traveling northbound on Danby Road when the jeep had to stop due to another vehicle in front of it attempting to make a turn. According to the troopers, the Toyota Yaris collided with the rear of the Jeep Patriot. It then went into oncoming traffic and was hit by a Cadillac CT6.
