Who is Michael Teutul?

Michael Teutul is an American television celebrity. People mostly recognize Michael Teutul for his appearance in the reality programs ‘Orange County Choppers’ and ‘American Chopper: Senior vs. Junior.’

Who are the parents of Michael Teutul?

Teutul was born as Michael Joseph Teutul in Suffern, New York on November 26, 1978. He was born to parents Paul Teutul Sr. and Paula Teutul.

Additionally, he has two brothers Paul Teutul Jr. and Daniel Teutul. He also has a sister Cristin Teutul. His father is the founder of Orange County Choppers.

He is of American nationality. Furthermore, there are no details available about his ethnicity at present.
