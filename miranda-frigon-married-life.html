Miranda Frigon Is In Matrimony With Her Spouse

August 2024 · 3 minute read

Actress and singer Miranda Frigon has made herself one of the recognizable faces on the Canadian television industry. With effort, dedication, and hard work she put on, no one can question the authenticity on her success.  

Taking her time off from acting for a while, at present, she is spending more time with her family. Without further ado, let’s learn more about how her life is going on these days.

The Canadian Actress’ Personal Life

The beautiful Miranda is already taken and is relishing a blissful married life with her husband, Ben Falcioni. The duo walked down the aisle in the presence of their families and close ones on September 13, 2015.

Celebrating the completion of four years since they exchanged the holy wedding vows, the lady shared exclusive photos of her wedding day on September 13, 2019. On it, she wrote:

“Four years ago, today, I married the most incredible man. I have never shared our wedding pictures, but today I was so happy, I was feeling so grateful for everything and everyone in my life. I wanted to celebrate my amazing husband, our love, our little girl, our amazing families, and all the people who have been a part of our journey. This was a very special day for you, and I just felt like you. Happy Anniversary to us!”

We wish that the actress will keep on maintain the same level of bond with her companion and get to celebrate many more Anniversary in the future.

Playing A Motherly Role In Real Life

By the looks of it, the October Kiss star is no doubt living a happy life. But she does not live alone with her partner; the duo is blessed with an adorable baby daughter.

As a mother, Frigon is incredibly happy and loves her kid abundantly, which we can see on her social media. Likewise, she frequently shares photos with her family on her social media.

Frigon’s Net Worth And Earnings

The lady holds a net worth of $1 million from her career in acting and singing. Her wealth is similar to another Canadian actress Liane Balaban.

The average salary of a Canadian actor earns $11.65 per hour and $22,718 in a year. In contrast, the singer makes $27180 in a year. 

The Pride singer is prominent for playing one of the lead stars in the Aurora Teagarden. In both dual television and movie show, she starred alongside Candance Cameron Bure, Niall Matter, Marilu Henner, Lexa Doig, and Peter Benson.
