FOCO WWE Limited Commemorative 1990s Attitude Era Mini Bobblehead Boxed Set Launches for Pre-Orde

June 2024 · 1 minute read

FOCO, aka Forever Collectibles, just revealed their new WWE limited commemorative 1990’s Attitude Era mini bobblehead boxed set for pre-order!

The Boxed Set

The set includes the following superstars:

  • Chyna
  • Rikishi
  • Kane
  • Mankind
  • Stone Cold Steve Austin
  • The Rock
  • Triple H
  • X-Pac

This set is limited to 360 pieces and costs $190. It is expected to ship by September 2023 as well.

You can currently pre-order them !
