Everything Braunwyn Windham-Burke Has Been Up To Since Leaving RHOC

June 2024 · 4 minute read

Since Braunwyn Windham-Burke only appeared on The Real Housewives of Orange County for seasons 14 and 15 and shared very intimate and personal parts of her life, fans wonder if she regrets starring on RHOC.

Braunwyn's friends were cast on RHOC and she is now off the series, but a lot is happening in her life even though she's no longer being followed by reality TV cameras. Let's take a look.

Braunwyn's Relationships

Braunwyn and Sean's marriage has been hard for some people to understand, but on Braunwyn's final season of RHOC, Braunwyn came out and shared that they were going to stay married but she would be dating women.

Braunwyn shared that she and Fernanda Rocha were in a relationship, but they broke up after a few months together. According to Us Weekly, Braunwyn said last year, “The first time that we went from friends to maybe a little more — and, like, barely more — was in February. We did not start dating until a month after she filed for divorce [from Tessa Rocha]. We can both date other people. She just got out of a marriage. We’re both in our forties. You know, we really love the time we spend together. She’s amazing. She’s an amazing person, but neither one of us is rushing into labels. … We don’t need to label [the time] we spend together.”

It's interesting to note that Fernanda was on RHOC back in season 6, as a "friend." Fernanda has her own training programs called Fernanda Rocha Fitness and also owns the Art of Fitness gym which is located in Laguna Beach, according to Hollywood Life.

Braunwyn was also in a relationship with Kris, and according to Page Six, they began their relationship in December 2020 after meeting via a dating app. In April 2021, the two broke up.

Moving To NYC

Moving to a new city is always exciting and also pretty nerve-wracking and stressful. It turns out that Braunwyn is moving to New York City.

According to E! News, Braunwyn said that she would be living in two places, suggesting that she would live in both NYC and California. Braunwyn said, "For me, it's becoming a New Yorker. I'm not just here for the week, I'm making a move. I'm going to be doing a dual situation."

Braunwyn explained that Sean thought it was a smart move and that he said their children would be okay with it. Braunwyn shared that after a Hawaii vacation, she was able to "find some clarity" and realized that she could be open to living in NYC half the time. This is inspiring to anyone who has wondered if they should change their life and take advantage of chances that have come their way.

Braunwyn said, "There's been some pretty amazing opportunities that have come to fruition in New York and it's just the right time." Braunwyn explained her excitement and said that her good friend Amy Schechter works with Million Dollar Listing star Ryan Serhant and she was excited to begin apartment hunting.

If anyone from RHOC was going to make a big move and change their life, it would definitely be Braunwyn, who always showed herself to be a warm and supportive person on the show. Braunwyn was always kind to her seven children and uses her social media for good, sharing support for the LGBTQ+ community. She's also a mental health advocate.

It will be interesting to see where Braunwyn Windham Burke's life takes her next, as fans are eager to hear what career opportunities she has in New York City.

Braunwyn had a lifestyle blog where she wrote about parenting before joining The Real Housewives of Orange County. According to Heavy.com, Braunwyn once wrote on the website, “My life is kinda ridiculous. Why am I doing this? Well, because people keep asking me to. Turns out, somewhere along the way of over procreation I’ve stumbled onto something kinda novel…enjoying it! I’m not totally sure where I’m going to take this, I’m sure it’ll change as I do, but I promise it’ll funny, irreverent, and honest, sharing what I love, hate and find amusing!!”
