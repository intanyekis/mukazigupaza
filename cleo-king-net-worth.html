Cleo King Wiki: Salary, Married, Wedding, Spouse, Family

Cleo King (born August 21, 1962) is an American actress probably best known for her roles as Aunt Lou in the HBO drama series Deadwood and Helene Parks in Boston Public. She is also known for portraying police officers, most notably Officer Garden in The Hangover, Penny the impound officer in a film with a similar premise to The Hangover, Dude, Where's My Car? and as the abrasive yet helpful school liaison officer in Pineapple Express.Born in St. Louis, Missouri, she has appeared in numerous films, television series, and commercials.She appears semi-regularly as Carl's grandmother, Rosetta McMillan "Nana", in the CBS sitcom Mike & Molly.King appeared as Neeta, the nanny of Jax Teller's son in the FX series Sons of Anarchy in 2009. She has also had roles in Lost, It's Always Sunny in Philadelphia, Eli Stone, The West Wing, Friends and Six Feet Under.She appeared in the film Transformers: Age of Extinction (2014). ﻿﻿﻿﻿﻿
