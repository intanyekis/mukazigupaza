Who is Quinton Aaron?

Quinton Aaron is an American actor.

He is mainly known for movies like The Blind Side (2009) and the TV series One Tree Hill (2011).

Quinton Aaron: Birth, Age, Parents, Ethnicity, Education

He was born on August 15, 1984, in the Bronx, New York City, New York, USA. His ethnicity is All-American.

His mother’s name is Laura Aaron but his father’s name is not available.

Later on, he moved to Augusta, Georgia after elementary school.

Health

In June 2019, Aaron was admitted to the hospital. The reason was he has an upper respiratory infection.

His fans were worried about his condition but he said that he is now feeling better.

Quinton Aaron: Professional Life, Career

Quinton Aaron made his acting debut in comedy-drama Be Kind Rewind in 2008, and his role as Micheal “Big Mike” Oher in the American semi-biographical sports drama film The Blind Side.

In 2010, he made his acting presence in an American series Law and Order.

In 2011, he was featured in television drama series named Harry’s Law, One Tree Hill, and The Hard Time Of RJ Berger.

He also appeared in the role of Simon in a thriller film named Left Behind.

Later on, In 2015, he appeared in movies named Halfway, What If, and Busy Day.

His recent acting projects are Busy Day (2017), Justice (2017), Bad Company (2018), and Fishbowl California (2018).

Awards and Nominations

In 2010 he was nominated for Black Reel under the category of Best Actor.

In the same year, he was nominated for Image Award for Outstanding Actor in a Motion Picture.

On 2017, he was nominated for Festival Award for Best Actor: Feature Film.

In 2010, he was nominated for MTV Movies Award for Best Breakout Star. In 2010, he was nominated for the Teen Choice Award for Choice Movie: Breakout Male.
