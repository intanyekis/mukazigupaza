Who Is Kathy Griffin Husband &amp; Support System Randy Bick?

June 2024 · 4 minute read

Randy Bick is the husband of comedian and actress Kathy Griffin. He is a Marketer Executive. Bick has also been involved in charitable activities. 

Even with his busy work schedule, he makes sure to support his wife at her standup and television appearances; he has even made a few cameos on her specials throughout their marriage. 

It’s clear that Randy provides financial stability to the couple and emotional support as well, establishing himself as a major element in Kathy Griffin’s successful career.

Kathy Griffin is an American comedian and television personality who has been entertaining her audience since the mid-1980s. She is best known for her award-winning stand-up comedy and her role on the Bravo reality show Kathy Griffin: My Life on the D-List. 

In recent years she has also become an outspoken advocate for civil rights, LGBT equality, women’s rights, and the preservation of the Affordable Care Act. 

Her combination of strong convictions with an irreverent sense of humor has made her an important voice in today’s social and political conversations. And through it all, she has maintained a loyal fan base that continues to grow with every project she takes on.

Kathy Griffin & Randy Bick’s relationship timeline 

Griffin, 62, has been dating marketing executive Randy Bick since 2012. The two parted ways in November 2018 and reconciled in April 2019.

On January 1, 2020, Griffin and Bick made the decision to get married in a ceremony officiated by comedian Lily Tomlin at her California home.

On New Year’s Eve, the 62-year-old comedian shared a video of herself and Bick telling their followers they were getting married in the wee hours of 2020.

Cheers to the new year, and surprise! The couple announced their impending nuptials together. Griffin said, “Tonight, around midnight,” and Bick added, “She said yes.”

She uploaded an update a few hours after midnight, saying: “The full ceremony was just under 14 minutes, but I had to offer you guys the uncut first 75 seconds. adore it We said it would be unusual. We can’t stop laughing because we are in love.”

The tweet featured a brief clip of the ceremony’s opening moments, in which Tomlin presided inside Griffin and Bick’s house.

Tomlin said that what was intended to be a brief, “toot it and boot it,” one-night stand has blossomed into something much more significant.

They remained a unit. After that, they were unable to resist each other.

Griffin wore a white dress with short sleeves and a black ribbon around the waist that was sparkling and had a modern look.

Griffin tweeted on their wedding day that “Bick had selected the particular dress for the ceremony since she had worn the same one on their first official date, which occurred in 2011.“

“I requested Randy to choose his favorite one of my long dresses, regardless of the reason. He chose this outfit and showed it to me along with this image of their first date. Once again, romance is hot,” she tweeted.

Griffin previously wed Matt Moline, who made an appearance on her reality series Kathy Griffin: My Life on the D-List, from 2001 to 2006.

Griffin has achieved tremendous success in her career, making her name known worldwide. She started as a comedian and quickly moved into stand-up routines, earning her spots on the Tonight Show and Late Show with David Letterman. 

Her fame grew further with her role as a flamboyant, gossiping correspondent on the television talk show “Fashion Police,” where she made candid remarks on celebrity fashion choices. 

Later on, Kathy starred in four different live comedy specials for “Bravo” which earned her two Emmy awards. Moreover, Griffin has also released a number of comedy albums that have topped the awards list time and time again.

Kathy Griffin’s stardom has grown from humble origins to widespread recognition thanks to her immense talent and comic wit.

Kathy’s recent revelation came as a shock to her fans 

Recently, Kathy was diagnosed with cancer, which shocked her fans. She has chosen to remain calm and positive during this difficult time, seeking treatment while continually showing her gratitude to those who have supported her throughout this journey. 

It is inspiring that she doesn’t let the diagnosis define who she is and remains determined to continue creating humorous moments for her fans. We know that with her strength and courage, Kathy will beat cancer and come out triumphant in the end.

“I’m about to go into surgery to have half of my left lung removed. Yes, I have lung cancer even though I’ve never smoked!” she revealed.

“The doctors are very optimistic as it is stage one and contained to my left lung. Hopefully, no chemo or radiation after this and I should have normal function with my breathing.”
