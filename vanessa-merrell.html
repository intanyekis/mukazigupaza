Vanessa Merrell is an American social media star, YouTuber, actress, singer, songwriter, musician, comedian, and content creator who is best known for being one-half of the sisterly duo Merrell Twins, alongside her identical twin Veronica Jo Merrell. She has also appeared in shows and movies such as Jane the Virgin, Hyperlinked, Like A Boss, Faking It, The Haunted House Contract, Where Is My Romeo?, The Standoff, Switched, Prom Night, and Mystery Twin Bin.
