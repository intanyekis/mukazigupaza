Drew Barrymore Can't Forgive Herself For Her Embarrassing Watch What Happens Live Interview

September 2024 · 6 minute read

Highlights

  • Drew Barrymore regrets her behavior during an appearance on Andy Cohen's show, citing that she drank too much.
  • Despite Barrymore's remorse, Cohen reassured her that she didn't owe him an apology.
  • Barrymore's embarrassing moments on Cohen's show didn't seem to bother anyone except herself.

During Andy Cohen's career, the TV host and producer has had his fair share of memorable moments. Between drinking too much on New Year's Eve and leaving everyone guessing when he claimed to be in love with John Mayer, Cohen knows how to get people's attention.

Of course, one of the keys to Cohen's success has been getting stars like Drew Barrymorewho are rich and famous to be more open with the public than usual. Sadly for Barrymore, however, she can't forgive herself for how she acted when Cohen interviewed her, even though he thinks she is overreacting.

Drew Barrymore Had Too Much To Drink On Watch What Happens Live

Now that Drew Barrymore is a talk show host herself, she must be learning how important it is to get guests to open up. After all, while Barrymore has had guests complain about her interviewing style, like Brooke Shields, many stars seem to love talking to her.

In March 2018, Barrymore made an appearance on Andy Cohen's Watch What Happens Live with her Santa Clarita Diet co-star Timothy Olyphant. In the aftermath of that interview, some of the things Barrymore said garnered attention, but that is almost always the case for Cohen's show.

If it wasn't for the fact that Barrymore later drew attention to her interview, her appearance almost certainly would have been mostly forgotten. However, when Cohen and Barrymore spoke again two years later, she put a huge spotlight on how she acted on Watch What Happens Live.

When Cohen appeared on Barrymore's behind-the-scenes series, Art of the Interview, in August 2020, the two TV hosts spoke about their previous interaction. During their conversation about Barrymore's Watch What Happens Live appearance, she explained why she will never forgive herself for how she acted.

“It was on your show, and I drank too much and I have never forgiven myself, nor will I ever forgive myself. Because, whatever personal thing I do, nobody sees it even though I’m an imperfect person, and I tout that, and I’ve had that liberty since I was a kid because it was all out there. And it was either boo hoo about it or say, ‘God, it’s sort of redeeming to not have to pretend to be perfect.’ But I really want to apologize to you because I really — I just, I will never let that happen again. I’m so sorry."

Once Barrymore drew attention to her Watch What Happens Live interview, some observers went back to take a second look at the most memorable moments. On a second viewing, it becomes pretty clear that Barrymore does seem like she may have had a few too many.

A perfect example of that is a clip of Barrymore talking about one of the most infamous moments of her career. During a 1995 appearance on his talk show, Barrymore infamously flashed Letterman while standing on his desk.

Andy Cohen Thought Drew Barrymore Was Being Too Dramatic

After Drew Barrymore explained to Andy Cohen why she couldn't forgive herself for drinking too much on his show, she immediately apologized to him. While it obviously is a good thing for people to apologize if they've done something wrong, Cohen's reaction to Barrymore's apology says a lot.

Instead of accepting Barrymore's apology, Cohen immediately told her she didn't owe him to begin with. More than that, Cohen was frank with Barrymore about how little impact her appearance on Watch What Happens Live had on him.

“I’m used to people being over-served on my show, and so I don’t even really have a totally clear memory of it, but you don’t need to apologize to me for being over-served on my show, that happens. You’re in a great club of people.”

Since Barrymore had just spoken about how impactful her 2018 Watch What Happens Live appearance was on her, Cohen's response could be insensitive. However, it seems very clear that Cohen was just trying to tell Barrymore that she could let her feelings about that interview go.

During an episode of The Drew Barrymore Show, the host revealed another time she was embarrassed because of drinking too much . After her guest Tituss Burgess revealed that he and Barrymore went on vacation together once, she remarked she was "too drunk" to remember that happening.

The truth is that, based on all the evidence, the only person who was bothered by Barrymore's conduct was her. After all, everyone knows that people enjoy Watch What Happens Live mostly because stars like Barrymore have much looser lips on that show.
