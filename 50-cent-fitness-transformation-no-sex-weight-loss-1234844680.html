50 Cent Shares Fitness Transformation, Credits No Sex For Weight-Loss

June 2024 · 2 minute read

50 Cent has left fans amazed at his sudden weight-loss transformation amid his New Years resolution to practice abstinence.

The G-Unit founder recently posted a series of photos that show a significant decrease in weight. The pictures, which were shared on Instagram, captured him dressed in various outfits, and were accompanied by spirited captions promoting his various endeavors and brands.

Fif’s new look comes weeks after he announced his intent to practice abstinence in 2024, as he looks to take his career and success to even greater heights.

“My new idea is so big, I don’t have time to be distracted,” the 48-year-old wrote at the time. “I’m practicing abstinence, I have been meditating and focusing on my goals. I hope this New Year helps you excel to the next level.”

This past weekend, the rapper recommended abstinence to his followers and others, as he credited his celibacy with allowing him to increase the intensity of his workouts. “I’m focused man, practicing abstinence is helping me train harder,” he wrote. “I feel great I think more people should try it.”

Fif’s slimmer physique caught the attention of his fans, as some noted the speed in which he appeared to be shedding pounds. Others cracked jokes regarding his weight-loss, such as him being just “a quarter” now in comparison to 50 Cent.

Last year, the Queens native vowed to get back in peak shape as he prepared to embark on his Final Lap Tour, promising to lose a huge chunk of weight before hitting the road. “We gonna do the tour and stuff like that, right? So I’m back working out like that,” he said on Instagram at the time.

“I always work out, but it’s different when I gotta start running and all that sh*t. Matter of fact, I’ma show y’all [on] Live every day. Yeah, I’ma do five miles in front of ’em every day.

“You gonna see me come back down. I’ma lose at least 25 pounds before [the tour]. I’ll be back in dog shape before I go. I’ma start talking mad sh*t when I’m back in shape. That’s why I be wanting to fight and sh*t like that, on the low.”
