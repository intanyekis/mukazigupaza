Holly Willoughby Says She Still Fancies Her Husband after 14 Years of Marriage

July 2024 · 3 minute read

Holly Willoughby, the popular co-presenter of ITV’s This Morning, has been married to her husband Dan Baldwin for around 14 years.

To be precise, she tied the knot with him on August 4, 2007.

The couple now has three children: two sons and a daughter.

The first of their children, Harry James Baldwin, was born on May 11, 2009, followed by their second child, Belle Baldwin, on April 14, 2011, and the third of their children, Chester William Baldwin, on September 29, 2014.

Holly Willoughby’s ‘Happy Relationship’ with Husband

While many relationships start losing their charm after couples become parents and marriages go over a decade, the same cannot be said for Willoughby and her husband’s wedding.

Speaking on Zoe Ball’s Radio 2 show in November 2021, the television personality implied that she was in a happy place in her marriage and still fancied her man of 14 years.

“I have a very happy relationship with my husband. I love him, I still fancy him - which is fantastic,” she gushed.

Holly Willoughby and her husband Dan Baldwin have a beach day. (Photo: Instagram)

Willoughby also elaborated that she had been looking inward the last few years since she felt something was missing.

“And I suddenly thought ‘I don’t know who I am, I don’t even know what music I like, I don’t know what my tastes are anymore,’” she said of her situation.

But at the same time, she was also happy to have a beautiful life, where she loved and fancied her spouse, had great children, and was blessed in many ways.

Interestingly, she once spiced up her relationship by wearing her new shoes in the bedroom. She shared the funny incident during an interview with Now magazine in 2009.

Times Holly Willoughby Gets Angry with Partner

The Dancing On Ice presenter, who is notoriously private about her family life, gets angry with her husband as well.

She shared that rare information about her relationship during a promotional tour for her book in late 2021.

In her book, Willoughby mentioned that she used to get disproportionately angry whenever she watched something on TV, and her partner questioned her choice of program.

“But actually, I’m angry because I subconsciously feel as though someone’s trying to control what I’m doing,” she further explained.

She went on to admit that she knew her spouse wasn’t trying to control her at all. Hence, she was worried about control in her life because she often got extremely strong reactions.

Holly Willoughby’s Spouse Dan Baldwin Is a TV Producer

Like her, Willoughby’s hubby also works in the television industry and regularly rubs shoulders with famous faces.

Baldwin is a successful television producer who has worked in shows such as Celebrity Juice, The Wheel, Virtually Famous, and Through the Key Hole.

He is also managing director at independent media company Hungry Bear, which was founded in 2014.

The media company has produced shows such as Michael’s McIntyre’s The Wheel and Bradley Walsh’s Breaking Dad.

Besides, he is the cousin of actress Tamzin Outhwaite, and eldest brother to James William, Dominic Anthony, and Francesca Maria Baldwin.
