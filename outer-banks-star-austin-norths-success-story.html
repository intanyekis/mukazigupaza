Meet Austin North: The Star Of Netflix's 'Outer Banks'

North’s ominous character, Topper, in the hit teen series Outer Banks on Netflix has gained him a massive fan following. He is now on the brink of becoming the next big star in Hollywood with Beautiful Disaster, a romantic comedy featuring Dylan Sprouse. A self-described introvert, the I Didn’t Do It star is also the founder of Honest Society, an exclusive clothing brand that caters to the urban crowd. However, his ultimate passion lies in music. Austin is an exceptional drummer and constantly uploads his music on streaming platforms. An avid traveler, he is known to explore unique cultures and countries during his free time. With a net worth of $5 million, the coming-of-age star is fulfilling his rite of passage in the entertainment world. Let us take a look at his lifestyle and goals.

As a young actor, Austin North takes a handsome paycheck for his villainous Outer Banks role. He made an estimated $30,000 per episode in season one. As for season three, he takes home approximately $50,000 per episode.

Destined To Be An Actor

Austin was born on July 30, 1996, in Cincinnati, Ohio. His family lived in Maineville and attended the Lebanon church. He was briefly schooled at the Lebanon Christian School before his family moved to Atlanta, Georgia, in 2006. He comes from a family of musicians, his elder sister Loren North, is a singer-songwriter based in Los Angeles, California, and is famous for her 2017 single Bad Timing, which has been streamed over 4 million times on Spotify. Growing up in a talented environment, Austin got interested in music early. He learned to play the drums and is an exceptional drummer. According to 1883 Magazine, music is his first passion, and the Outer Banks confesses to starting a band if not being famous for acting.

But destiny played its part, and he was discovered in a talent showcase at 12. During his late teens, he landed his first leading role in Disney Channel's popular comedy series I Didn't Do It, which premiered in 2014. The launchpad into the television industry gave the young actor much recognition. He starred in leading Disney productions, including Disney's A.N.T. Farm, Kickin' It on Disney XD, Nickelodeon's See Dad Run, and ABC's current seasons of General Hospital. However, his breakthrough role came in 2020 while being cast in the hit Netflix drama series Outer Banks.

The Phenomenal Success Of 'Outer Banks' 

North's character Topper Thornton in the sensational hit Netflix series Outer Banks instantly gained worldwide fame. The show premiered in April 2020 amidst lockdown and immediately dominated the viewership charts. According to The Wrap, Season 1 peaked at No. 4 on the Top 10 Netflix list clocking 35.1 million hours viewed. In comparison, Season 2 stayed put on the No. 7 slot with 23.8 million hours viewed as the global fans stayed hooked on the adventurous treasure hunt plot and with a heady mix of love triangles. Season 3 premiered on Netflix on February 23, 2023, and it broke all the previous viewership records by clocking 154.97 million hours viewed. The streaming giant has recently renewed the highly successful drama series for Season 4, making it one of the most-watched television series.

According to The Things, North took an estimated paycheck of $30,000 per episode in season one. By season three, the figure doubled, and he made an estimated $50,000 per episode. The lucrative series has opened the floodgates of acting projects for the See Dad Run actor. He will soon be seen in a Hollywood film opposite Disney alum Dylan Sprouse in the upcoming film Beautiful Disaster.

Joining The A-lister Club

Austin is joining the ranks of Hollywood A-listers with his acting prowess, and he has been offered a role in the underproduction romantic comedy Beautiful Disaster. According to Deadline, the film is directed by the famed director of Cruel Intentions and After we Collided, Roger Kumble. After finding success in a villainous role on Outer Banks and signing an A-league film, North’s net worth has surged to approximately $5 million.

The born musician is also known to exhibit keen entrepreneurial skills. He launched a clothing line named Honest Society in 2021. It features the latest streetwear trends from global fashion scenes. The online store sells sweatpants, hats, graphic hoodies, t-shirts, and socks. Austin’s personal style file inspires the curated capsule collection and carries his trademark passion for creating an expressive outlet for art. The A.N.T. Farm actor also wishes to start a podcast show in the future to discuss pressing societal issues and bring in celebrity guests to talk about the things that make them vulnerable as humans. He is also planning to create a music band that will allow him to travel and explore various cultures.

Austin North is a talented actor with a cute and handsome boy-next-door charm. With his out-of-the-box success, he has capitalized on his fame and is on the brink of becoming the young heartthrob of Hollywood.
