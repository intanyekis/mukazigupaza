Davis Cleveland played Cece Jones's brother Flynn Jones in all three seasons of Shake It Up. He was eight years old when he was cast in the role and a pre-teen when the show ended in 2013. Fans want to know all about Davis Cleveland's life, including whether he kept acting after Shake It Up and his true feelings about the popular series.

What Did Davis Cleveland Do After Shake It Up?

Now that Shake It Up and other shows are available on Disney+, fans feel nostalgic and rewatch their favorite episodes. Flynn Jones is a particularly fun character because he's so smart. He became such a popular part of Shake It Up that viewers have always been curious about Davis Cleveland.

Although some Disney Channel kids like to leave the industry entirely, Davis Cleveland seems to have wanted to continue acting. He was cast in a few different Disney shows. In 2010 and 2011, he played Roy Waffles on Zeke and Luther. He then played some small roles in Hannah Montana and Good Luck Charlie.

Cleveland is most known for playing Manny in the Nickelodeon movies Rufus and Rufus 2, which aired in 2016 and 2017. The actor reprised this role in the 2023 movie Rufus 3.

The franchise has a magical plot that definitely appeals to kids. Manny doesn't have a big friend group, and he wishes that he had someone to play with. He comes across a dog collar that ends up having amazing powers. After his dog Rufus wears the collar, Rufus becomes a kid who is Manny's age.

Since Davis Cleveland was so young when he starred in Shake It Up, it appears that he became a regular kid when the show ended. His Instagram account shows that he has been having fun with his friends. In 2019, he went to his prom, and previously, he also shared photos of himself on a sports team.

He seems to have achieved what many kids hope to: a nice balance between regular life and exploring his love for performing. Instead of giving up his place in the industry entirely, he took on a few roles but still went to a real school.

The actor talked about Shake It Up when he was at D23 Expo 2011, and he also mentioned that starring on a TV show meant being homeschooled. It sounds like he wanted to experience what being in regular school would be, and he definitely did. He looks thrilled in every photo that he posts on Instagram, which proves that this was the right choice for him.

According to a video on J-14's YouTube Channel, Cleveland became a student at Texas A&M University in 2021. He hasn't posted anything about his college life on Instagram, though. It's possible that he wants to have some privacy now.

How Did Davis Cleveland Feel About Starring On Disney's Shake it Up?

If starring in a TV show is a totally miserable experience, it's only logical that an actor would want to step away from the industry. Thankfully, that doesn't seem to be the case for Davis Cleveland.

While the actor hasn't been interviewed as a young adult, he has only said great things about his experience on Shake It Up. Anytime he was asked about the show when he was a child, he was happy to share something positive.

According to the Shake It Up Wiki Fandom, Cleveland liked being on Shake It Up. As he said in a 2011 interview with Good Morning America, "It's the best experience ever. It's like Disney World every morning."

The actor was interviewed at D23 Expo 2011, and the video is available on YouTube for fans to check out. He was in a good mood and danced throughout the interview, which was adorable to see. He said that it was fun attending the event and that he liked meeting different people.

Cleveland mentioned an episode of Shake It Up that he loved filming and said, "One of the really fun ones is Flynn, my character, actually believes that his next door neighbor is an alien. We get a really cool guest star, I don't want to tell you who he is but he's really nice."

While Cleveland loved starring on Shake It Up, his co-stars seemed to have enjoyed working with him. When Zendaya was interviewed about Shake It Up by Assignment X in 2013, she called Cleveland "hilarious."

When asked about his behavior while filming Shake It Up, Zendaya had tons of praise. She said, "He is just like that character. Honestly, Davis I’m 100% convinced that he is not 10 years old. There is like a little 30-year-old man stuck inside of him. I hang out with him like a teenager and any of my other friends and he is so smart. He is so beyond his years and it’s actually pretty astounding to me because he is so smart. I love being around him because he is such a cool kid!"

Since Davis Cleveland starred in Rufus 3, which came out in January 2023, it seems that he is happy to act when he gets the chance. It's inspiring that he was able to maintain some of his acting career while still going to school. While he hasn't said if he will keep acting after he finishes college or if he will choose another career, it will be cool to see where he ends up.
