Megan Thee Stallion &amp; Yung Miami Kiss After BET Awards

July 2024 · 2 minute read
Los Angeles, CA - 

The City Girls hosted a 2021 BET Awards afterparty on Sunday night (June 27) and Megan Thee Stallion pulled up to celebrate her big night where she took home three trophies. A clip from the party has gone viral overnight of the Houston Hottie and Yung Miami sharing a brief intimate kiss.

Yung Miami and Megan Thee Stallion had to know the cameras were on with the flashing lights flickering as they went in to lock lips and then laugh it off. The City Girls linked up with Meg in 2020 for her raunchy Good News track “Do It on the Tip.”

Quality Control hosted a City Girls Celebration Dinner afterparty at an undisclosed Los Angeles location following the award show which also saw Chloe Bailey of Chloe x Halle in attendance as well.

Megan Thee Stallion and Yung Miami both hit the stage as performers at the award show. The City Girls performed their steamy “Twerkulator” summer banger while Meg joined DJ Khaled during his set but made sure to exit stage right before DaBaby made his guest appearance.

Megan Thee Stallion won Best Female Hip Hop Artist while also emerging victorious in the Best Video and Best Collaboration categories for her chart-topping “WAP” collab with Cardi B.
